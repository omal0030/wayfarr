New York Map

Expanding a map into a new tab will allow for better searching. Searching will display anything listed for the restaurant: cuisine type, friend who recommended, anything in the notes section, zip code, etc.

Searching will give you the first three results and then you can click on “See all XX results” to see everything.

In that new tab, you can also star the map (right below search bar) to allow you to access this on your phone. Once you have starred a map:

1. Access Google Maps

2. Press hamburger menu

3. Press “Your Places”

4. Press “Maps”

5. Press “MAP NAME"

Shared restaurant map of Seattle, WA.

While in Google Maps on your phone:

Click “View Map Legend” to view filtering options.

After you select the filters of your choosing, press the back button top left (not refresh).

Click on a pin to see all details of the restaurant, get directions, and to “View Map Legend.”

The searching function on mobile does not work for the map, it will function as Google Maps normally does.

Shared restaurant map of Seattle, WA.

Map Colors:

Blue: one of your friends has recommended that place.

Green: two or more of your friends has recommended that place.

Red: one or more of your friends do not recommend that place.

Yellow: restaurants people have not been to, but would like to try.
